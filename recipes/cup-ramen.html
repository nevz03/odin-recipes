Cup Ramen

ARE YOU READY?

FOR THE QUICKEST RECIPE!

THIS IS CUP RAMEN!! NOM NOM NOM!!

Ingredients

  • 1 unit/cup of Cup Ramen.
  • Water (I know!! Who knew?!!

Steps

  1. Boil your water.
  2. After it's boiled, open the cup ramen and pour the boiled water until you see indicated line inside.
  3. Close the lid for 3 minutes
  4. OPEN AND ENJOY!!!
