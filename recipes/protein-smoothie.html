My Protein Smoothie

Check below for the recipe

Are you looking to bulk up? Me too!

Here's an easy delicious recipe that will help get them GAINS

Ingredients

  • Whey Protein Chocolate - 1 scoop
  • Medium Banana - 1
  • Peanut Butter - 2 tbs
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Shredded Coconut - a handful

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of milk to your blender, then the banana ( break it in half)
  2. Put in the Whey Chocolate Protein, followed by the Peanut butter and then finally the Shredded Coconut!
  3. Blend that thing!
  4. Pour that godlike goodness into a cup or whatever and ENJOY!
