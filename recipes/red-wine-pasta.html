Red Wine Pasta

Read below for more

Here lies a tantalizing experience programmed to send you to heaven!

You have to try this at least once in your life and I assure you, that you will not regret it! This dish infuses the pasta with the wine, thus give such a magnificent taste. Perfect for date night!

Ingredients

  • Red wine(Cabernet or Malbec is best)- 4 cups
  • Spaghetti (more visually appealing) - 1 lb
  • Garlic, thinly sliced cloves - 4
  • Butter - 2 tbs
  • Grated parmesan cheese - 1 cup
  • Parsley (optional, for garnish) - 1/2 cup

Steps

  1. Measure 1/4 cup (60ml) of wine and set aside
  2. Combine the water and the remainder of the bottle of wine in a large pot and bring to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, add spaghetti and cool(stirring frequently) until about 7 minutes.
  4. Reserve 1/4 cup(60ml) of the cooking liquid and drain the spaghetti. Set aside.
  5. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil on medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the reserved cooking liquid and reserved wine to the skillet and bring it to a simmer. Add the pasta and toss to coat with the sauce.
  7. Add the butter and stir until it melts into the pasta.
  8. Add salt, pepper , parmesan and parsley. Stir to combine.
  9. ENJOY!!!
